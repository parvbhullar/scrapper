Background

Summary

CEO at Strawbees, an educational construction toy for makers and mad scientists of all ages.

Prior to Strawbees, I spent 7+ years as a management consultant. I began at EY Advisory, moved to Accenture and then I spent a few years at McKinsey&Company. The diversity of the three firms implied varied type of studies; From operational, to strategic topics. Experiencing both types of challenges, and particularly when mixed, has given me great understanding of how to address business problems, and how to prioritize.

Educationally, I have a Masters degree in Industrial Engineering & Management, from Linköping Institute of Technology. I speak English, Swedish (plus Norwegian and Danish), and have worked in more than 40 countries across Europe, Asia, North America and Africa.
