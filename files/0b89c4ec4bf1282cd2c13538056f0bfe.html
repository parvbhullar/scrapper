Background

Summary

Mike Robon joined Safeco, a member of the Liberty Mutual Group, in 2003 as a Senior Product Manager for the Southeastern US. In 2005 he became a Vice President in the product management organization responsible for the western US. In 2008 Robon assumed leadership of the national auto product management group. Robon held this role through July 2012. At this time, he was named the Senior Vice President for Liberty Mutual’s $5.2 billion national property insurance program.

Prior to Liberty Mutual, Robon was a product manager for Progressive in both Houston and Denver, where he also sat on the board of the Rocky Mountain Intermountain Insurance Organization. Outside of insurance, he worked as an analytical marketing research consultant and helped a regional bank reorganize and launch information-driven products.

Robon holds a bachelor’s degree in Economics from Northwestern University and an MBA from Carnegie Mellon University with emphasis in statistics, finance, marketing and entrepreneurship. He was on the Board of Directors for the Washington Community Alliance for Self Help, a non-profit organization that, through the teaching of life skills, promotes self-sufficiency through entrepreneurship. He also was part of a team advising the city of Kirkland with their 20 year plan for the Highland's neighborhood.

Specialties: Product management, process management, regulatory relations, pricing, distribution strategy, product design, contract design, competitive analysis and more.
