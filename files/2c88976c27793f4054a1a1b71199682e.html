Background

Summary

I'm a Content Marketing Strategist and co-founder of SPROUT Content, where we help B2B companies find their voice, tell their story and grow their business by creating exceptional content assets. As a Hubspot partner and content marketer, I leverage technology and marketing to set measurable goals and develop winning content strategies that increase website traffic, generate leads and boost sales. Specialties include:

◾Organic SEO and keyword strategies
◾Lead Generation and DRIP campaigns
◾Inbound marketing strategy and implementation
◾ SEO website copy and social media content

As the chief content strategist at SPROUT Content, I enable brands to use their website as a 24/7 sales tool and position themselves as thought leaders in their industry. If you are looking for a savvy inbound marketing strategy to build or sustain a competitive advantage, check out our client results http://www.sproutcontent.com/results. We pride ourselves on being an authentic, collaborative content marketing agency that creates content delivering measurable ROI.

Before SPROUT Content:

I love my content strategy role at SPROUT Content and I'm very appreciative of prior work experience that got me here, including being the Marketing Director for AppRiver, an email SAAS provider, SEO content developer at Plugit.com, an web development company and PR account executive at Saatchi & Saatchi and General Motors Europe. I have a MBA from the University of West Florida and a BS in PR (always fun to say) from University of Florida... Go Gators!
