Background

Summary

I am currently looking for full time position as a research scientist.

My research project in Prof. Chulsung Bae's research group at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, focused on the synthesis and development of acid functionalized ion-conducting polymer materials for applications in energy conversion devices. In particular, I was interested in synthesis of proton exchanage membrane (PEM) by modifying the commercially available "block copolymers". For this purpose, I utilized organic synthetic route with a primary focus on transition metal catalyzed carbon—carbon bond forming reactions.
