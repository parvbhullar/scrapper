Background

Summary

Pharmacologist | Metabolic Diseases | Diabetes | Obesity | Scientific Leadership | Resource Management

Holger has 15+ years of in–depth disease expertise and drug discovery & development experience in the areas of metabolic diseases and endocrinology. He is experienced in all preclinical stages spanning small molecules, protein, and antibody therapeutics. Holger has built and led critical internal and external cross-functional project teams and collaborations in complex matrix systems for Fortune 100 companies, including Eli Lilly & Co. He is an influential leader in defining strategies and disease landscapes/drug portfolios related to obesity, diabetes, and disease complications as well as providing expertise for due diligence investigations.

Holger has a strong record of multi-disciplinary team building and has been characterized as an energizing, engaging, innovative, and result-driven scientific leader. His value–based leadership style supports employee development by offering opportunities for personal growth and performance improvement.

Career highlights include:
• Developed target platforms; identified and championed new drug targets and decision–making biomarkers in three therapeutic areas; advanced candidates to reach portfolio milestones.
• Led approaches to identify and validate new drug targets using large–scale RNAi screens.
• Spearheaded the infrastructure of state–of–the–art metabolic diseases research teams and laboratories and was key in strategy definition during formation and reorganization of new research department.
• Pioneered “translational sciences” R&D approaches to better understand disease pathology in humans leading to the discovery of more promising disease intervention points.
• Discovered novel MOA of an already marketed heart drug for diabetes therapeutic area resulting in drug repositioning, a projected multimillion-dollar sales increase, and the initiation of a new drug development program.
• Recognized academic scientist.
