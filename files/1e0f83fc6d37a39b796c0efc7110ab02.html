KCRW Media Player(Link)

January 2009 – January 2010

About the Client
KCRW (89.9 and KCRW.com) is the number one rated public radio station in Southern California. The station hosts one of the nation’s largest collections of locally produced, nationally distributed talk program content. KCRW also features an eclectic mix of music, news, information, and cultural programming.

The Challenge
Digitaria was tapped to develop a custom media player for KCRW to accommodate personalized listening experiences for KCRW’s more than 550,000 person-strong weekly listener base. Digitaria was then later asked to redesign KCRW’s homepage along with creating their new Eclectic 24 landing page.

Our Solution
With the redesign, Digitaria emphasized content exploration through KCRW’s player functionality. The player allows users to access playlists, subscribe to podcasts, embed content on blogs, and purchase books and CDs from the author/artist interviewed by KCRW. Another feature lets listeners and users share links to favorite programs with friends and family.
