Summary

Marketing manager with 10 years experience in leading International Fashion, Lifestyle and Sports Apparel brands. With a strong affinity for fashion, boardsports and lifestyle, the objective is achieving the Company’s targets to drive the brand’s image, products & communication development.

I have started my career in the Marketing department of the brands O’Neill, Speedo Swimwear, Vision Streetwear and Puma ten years ago and due to a great affinity with the fashion, lifestyle and sports markets, started since an early stage progressing to roles with increased responsibility in the areas of Marketing, Communication, PR and Athlete management.
In the last two years at O'Neill, I have been consolidating my experience in the Commercial area through the sales planning, sell in, and forecasting of the O’Neill Collections.

Since December 2014, I've started to work at Fashion's biggest Retailer; H&M, as Marketing and PR Manager in Portugal.
