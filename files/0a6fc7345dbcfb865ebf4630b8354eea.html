Background

Summary

Proven entrepreneurial business and sales leader, marketer and co-founder of 3 awesome daughters. Public writer and speaker, primarily on topics related to digital marketing and business, bringing a refreshing injection of light-heartedness and humor to what can be a dry topic!

In my current position at Chango, I am the Chief Product Officer, and formerly held the role of Chief Revenue Officer. As a co-founder I joined pre-revenue and built a sales organization that exceeded revenue targets for 15 consecutive quarters, designed industry-defining solutions and oversee a marketing team that is one of the most respected in the industry today.

Request a copy of our magazine, The Programmatic Mind at chango.com/resources

Prior to Chango, I founded and led the iCrossing / Hearst global digital media group, developing the concept of 'performance display', an innovative planning strategy that drove significant ROI from display advertising for some of the world's largest and coolest brands.

Additionally, DaxThink is my umbrella for a number of experimental projects.

I have 15+ practical years in the digital space with experience in just about everything online, including media planning, media buying, retargeting, digital marketing strategy, usability, creative, technical management, affiliate marketing and making clients really, really happy.

Passionate and successful at building new businesses and evolving existing ones. Living on a plane between San Francisco, Denver, Toronto and London.
