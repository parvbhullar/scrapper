Background

Summary

For over two decades I've helped clients address their financial concerns and develop sound strategies by focusing on each individual's specific needs. With extensive experience throughout major shifts in the markets, I have dedicated my career to developing the personalized investment planning services that my clients and their families deserve. I work with high net worth individuals, families, and businesses to provide integrated wealth management and planning services. Integrity, objective advice, attention to detail, and unwavering concern for the wellbeing of my clients are the hallmark of my career and my clients service model.

I began my career as a Financial Advisor in 1985 with International Assets Advisory Corp, LLC an Investment Banking Brokerage Firm in Winter Park, Florida, that specializes in global asset allocation. I earned a G.G., Graduate Gemologist, at the prestigious Gemological Institute of America in Residence, in Santa Monica, CA, in 1982.

I have a passion to "maximize the quality of my clients lives as they define it." Please visit my website at http://home.wellsfargoadvisors.com/michael.spartz for more information about my practice and the services I provide to my clients.
