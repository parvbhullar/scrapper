Background

Summary

As a Senior Director of Research and Development and former Senior Director of IT I have had the pleasure of leading a talented group of individuals through many journeys.

I have an excellent understanding of leadership, business process, technology, software development, data analytics, and data management. I passionately believe that information is critical for achieving business success, and that consumable information, when combined with an innovation approach to applied technologies, provides a means to achieving an effective competitive advantage on a global scale.

I believe in leadership from the heart, i.e. successful leaders must understand their staff, know how to keep them motivated and on task, and be willing to lead from the front rather than from the corner office. During my career I have successfully led the development of new technologies and the delivery of complex software offerings, including:

-Development and delivery of SAS’s Environment Manager Operational Intelligence offering, designed to address the operational rigor and needs of Enterprise Class applications. The offering includes a unified Data Mart, event forensics, correlations, exception analysis, linear models, and time series forecasting.

-Development and delivery of a patented, award-winning SaaS product that manages thousands of virtual sessions and is now considered the first implementation of Cloud computing at SAS.

-Creation of the Center for IT Analytics, a cross division working group focused on leveraging IT domain expertise for the benefit of SAS’s products and the development of a collective set of "Fit For IT" best practices.

Specialties & interests: SAAS, Big Data, data analysis, forecasting, predictive analytics, event management capacity planning, Data Marts, grids, cloud computing, virtualization, Groovy, & Hadoop.

Publications

Log entries, Events, Performance Measures, and SLAs: Understanding and Managing your SAS® Deployment by Leveraging the SAS® Environment Manager Data Mart(Link)

SGF Conference
March 2014

SAS Environment Manager is included within the release of SAS 9.4. This exciting new product allows administrators to monitor the performance and operation of their SAS deployments. What very few people are aware of is that the data collected by SAS Environment Manager is stored in a centralized data mart that is designed to help administrators to better understand the behavior and performance of the components of their SAS solution stack.

This data mart can also be used to help organizations meet their IT Infrastructure Library (ITIL) reporting and measurement requirements. In addition to the information about alerts, events and performance metrics collected by the SAS Environment Manager agent technology, this data mart includes the metadata audit and content usage data previously only available from the SAS® Audit and Performance Measurement (APM) package.
