Background

Summary

Senior executive with 20+ years of experience in the design and development of customer-focused software-based products and solutions. Career accomplishments highlighting revenue growth and innovation include:
• Executive sponsor and innovator of CardValet mobile banking and card control application, providing debit and credit cardholders the ability to control when and where their payment cards are used.
• Product Development leader of Debit Card, Credit Card, ATM, Risk product lines responsible for generating $400M in annual revenue. Exceeded 2013 new product revenue by 100% $4M vs. $2M plan.
• Managed annual investment of $10 million in new products and enhancements.
• Designed and developed the award winning UChoose Rewards loyalty program allowing small financial institutions to compete with national FIs. Increased loyalty revenue 200% from 2008 to 2010.
• Designed and prototyped a biometric-based smart card medical information solution for the Mexican Ministry of Health
• Implemented best practices in project management, requirements management, software development, and process improvement, resulting in improved client satisfaction, fewer bugs, less rework, and greater profitability.
