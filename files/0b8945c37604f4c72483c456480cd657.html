Background

Summary

I am a Managing Director for CBIZ MHM, LLC where I am the National Practice Leader for the Federal Tax Credits and Incentives practice. I am located in the firm's Orange County, California office. I also lead the Research & Development (R&D) Tax Credit Services Group.

With more than 24 years of experience in public accounting and tax, I have served a variety of businesses in the manufacturing, software, high tech, construction, professional service and not-for-profit industries. My primary focus is in R&D tax credit services and other tax incentives.

I have conducted over 450 research credit studies for numerous companies in various industries, and have served as a representative before the IRS and state taxing authorities to support and defend numerous research credit claims for taxpayers.

I also have experience in financial accounting, reporting and management, auditing, and individual and business income tax. I have supervised compilations, audits and reviews for various clients, including small and medium sized businesses, in addition to providing income tax planning, consulting and compilation services.

As a CPA certified in California, I am a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants and the California Society of Certified Public Accountants.

Throughout my career I have authored several publications and conducted numerous presentations on current tax legislation, R&D tax credit issues and other tax related business incentives. I have also spoken to organizations such as the California Society of CPA’s, the National Association of Manufacturers, the R&D Credit Coalition in Washington, D.C., and the American Bar Association.

Specialties: Research and Development Tax Credits, Manufacturing Incentives and Credits, Construction, Architecture, Energy Incentives and Credits, Green Credits, Software, Food Processors and Manufacturers, High-Tech, Life Science, SALT Services
