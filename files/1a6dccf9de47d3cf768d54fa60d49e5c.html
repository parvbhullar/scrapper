Projects

CSR Youth Ambassador Campaign-Engaging 25 Million Colleague Students from China on Sustainability(Link)

June 2013

I have taken the lead to initiate and launch a nationwide CSR Youth Ambassador Annual Campaign since 2013, striving to raise awareness of "corporate social responsibility" and "corporations and society" among Chinese youth and 25 million colleague students. The winners of this campaign will have the opportunity to dialogue directly with CEOs of leading Chinese companies and make suggestions on a variety of topics including environment protection, protect worker's rights, supply chain sustainability, and corporation's impact to the community. The Youth are encouraged to make public statements advocating sustainability as youth ambassadors, such as combating climate change, becoming a responsible consumer and represent Chinese youth to attend China-Japan-Korea Youth Roundtables.

Established Global Compact Local Network in Sudan- Fostering Partnerships and Contributing to Peace and Development(Link)

January 2008 – December 2010

Launched a Global Compact Network in Sudan in Khartoum in December 2008. Mobilized more than 150 senior representatives of business, the UN, government and civil society to join the launch and fostered open dialogues and learning among international organizations, governments, investors, multinationals in Sudan, international and local NGOs and local communities. The efforts to establish Sudan network aims to provide a practical platform for all actors to explore how business can make a positive contribution to sustainable development and conflict mitigation. Historically for the first time the UN Global Compact and its ten principles was introduced officially in Sudan with large scale, and the platform established will provide all stakeholders with more opportunities to share practical experiences and successful stories from the country, and to steer the potential partnership projects towards the priority needs of the country, contributing to peace and sustainable development.

Guidance on Responsible Business in Conflict-Affected & High-Risk Areas: A Resource for Companies & Investors(Link)

December 2008 – December 2011

Mobilized major Multinational Companies from emerging markets, including CNPC(China), Sinopec(China), PetroNas (Malaysia), ONGC(India), 's participation in the 3-year project of “Responsible Investment and Business Practices in Conflict-affected and High-Risks Areas” and ongoing activities and developed the guidance on responsible business mainly targeted to companies and investors with business interests/operations in high-risk operating environments. The value of the Guidance has received many positive feedback from various stakeholders. A pilot group of companies and investors willing to use/implement the Guidance was established and will report back on lessons learned.
