Background

Summary

My passion is for creation. I love stories and games and the creation of both. Games allow us to experience media today in a way unlike any other, letting us interact with the fiction and with each other, and have authorship of our own experiences.

My greatest strengths lie in narrative and system design, which I studied on both a personal and academic level before entering the field. I now have experience in the development of small and large projects, for platforms ranging from handhelds to PC games. I am enthusiastic about depth-focused game design and creating deliberate, sustainable immersion, paired with reliable challenge-and-reward feedback cycles.
