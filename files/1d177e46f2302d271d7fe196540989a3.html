Background

Summary

Annette Bubak
SolarCity, Builder Sales Manager
Las Vegas Quality Realty, REALTOR®, GREEN
Nevada ENERGY STAR Partners/GREEN Alliance - President

After five years of being in high performance home and building contracting and consulting business, Bubak enters the solar industry by joining SolarCity as their Las Vegas Builder Sales Manager. Primary focus is working with home builders in adding solar to the new home industry as a clean source of energy and especially meeting the demand of home owners.

A 20-year veteran of the real estate sales and marketing industry and a GREEN Designated REALTOR®, Bubak is committed to educating real estate professionals of the benefits of high performance homes and GREEN certified homes and buildings. As a NAR Green Council GREEN Designation instructor, Bubak is dedicated to preparing the real estate professionals.

As a founding member and currently serving as the president of Nevada ENERGY STAR® Partners - GREEN Alliance, a 501 c 3 charitable nonprofit organization. In partnership with U.S. EPA and U.S. DOE, national, state, and local public/private partnerships is dedicated to thriving, sustainable communities. Through collaborative education and community awareness initiatives the non-profit creates a commitment to conservation, resource preservation and healthy, sustainable living in Nevada. Bringing signature events to Nevada such as southern Nevada's signature Earth Day event - GREENFest.

Bubak runs her own consulting company, Bubak Consulting, where she brought her decades of experience to the market place after serving in a variety of executive marketing and sales positions for such Southern Nevada.

Past VP of Better Building Performance, a residential and commercial energy performance general contractor specializing in energy assessments, retrofit, HVAC and solar installation services. Bubak was principal and president of Distinct ENERGY Performance a consulting firm for energy efficiency in homes and buildings.

Specialties:Finally, Bubak is a 2011 Women's Hall of Fame Leadership Inductee, NAHB-Institute of Residential Marketing CSP and CMP and Nevada ENERGY STAR® Partners‚ Board of Directors, Advisory Committee of HomeFree Nevada, Green Chips-Convene for Green, NV-A-Bet Board of Directors, LVCC Green Initiative and Member of the GLVAR. Past member of SNHBA- Green Building Development Committee, Sales and Marketing Council, and SNWA Water Smart Coalition, LVHCA Governing Board/Sales and Marketing Council.
