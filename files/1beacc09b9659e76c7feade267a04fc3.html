Business Founder/CEO/President, Digital Strategist & Expert Columnist/Author

WebAdvantage.net
April 1998 – July 2013 (15 years 4 months)Greater Baltimore

Back in 1998 when everyone else was saying, “Build it and they will come,” I founded Web Ad.vantage because I knew that businesses would need to do more than just build a website in order to grow sales and revenues online -- they needed *marketing*.

Having worked client-side before creating my company, it’s always been important to me to play a “client advocate” role -- in other words, to be pragmatic and strategic rather than merely tactical. Anyone can do tactics; not everyone does them responsibly. I think our longevity speaks to our ability to deliver results.

Since those pioneering early days, Web Ad.vantage has continued to evolve. We now find ourselves more in the role of navigator, helping clients chart a course through the ever-more complex world of digital marketing. We deliver equal doses of assessments, audits, and analysis as we do tactical execution. Some of the additional concrete ways in which we help our clients include:

• Digital strategic guidance/gap analysis
• Business and/or competitive digital intelligence
• Lead generation
• Quick ad creative/message testing
• Content Marketing
• Search, Social, Email, Local, Mobile & Video digital marketing/advertising strategies
• In-house training for digital marketing skills
