Background

Summary

People and technology are my greatest passions. I strive to "Be Awesome" with everyone in my life, and to wield technology like a wizard.

Until recently, I worked at FullContact, a Denver startup aiming to solve the world's contact information problem. At FullContact, I built and managed several key services that supported a wide array of contact management products. Among the most notable was a real-time service which combined multiple contacts from differing sources into a single contact, additionally syncing the data back to writable sources. In addition to this, I also managed the Core service which provided programmatic access to all the contact data to end-user clients as well as internal service clients.

I am highly motivated by the people that I work with and am always willing to lend a hand. I feel comfortable in team environments and can work with a variety of personalities. I find great joy in learning, discussing, and applying new technologies - especially with groups of people.
