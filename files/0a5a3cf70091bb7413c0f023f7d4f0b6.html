Background

Summary

Courses

Projects

Ny Reskontro for Telenor Norway

September 2011 – July 2013

Project Management. The purpose of the project was to merge receivables for Mobile, Fixed Private, Fixed Business and Wholesale Fixed to a common ledger and replace 14 year old modifications with an upgrade to standard functionality in Oracle eBusiness Suite Release 12. This gives large reductions in operating costs. The system handles yearly payments of 30 billion NOKs. Accenture was the system supplier, primarily with Indian offshore resources.

Posten HR

January 2007 – September 2008

Project Manager and overall responsible for testing of a new personnel system for Posten Norge, including end-to-end and regression test for all corresponding systems. The personal system was based on Oracle E-Business Suite and was the first time such a complex system was built on this technology in Norway and Europe. The project was divided into two streams; HR Payroll, which was successfully implemented in 2007, and other HR functions implemented in 2008. It was a demanding project; still the project team received third place for "Team of the Year 2007" by Capgemini.

Løft Modulis

October 2008 – September 2009

Project Manager and overall responsible for testing in the LØFT Modulis project, a 300 million development project. Capgemini, Sogeti and Software Innovation were responsible for delivering the solution to Lånekassen, where the aim was to obtain a case management system making it easier to change the rules on student loans, and improved network solutions for the customers. Had a team of test coordinators, technical testers and functional testers. Sogeti's method TMap (R) was used as test methodology. The project followed Scrum methodology, but delivery challenges resulted in changed implementation strategy and processes.

OK-MAN (Oslo Kommune – Metropolitan Area Network)

October 2009 – October 2010

Project Manager and overall responsible for test of OK-MAN (Oslo Kommune – Metropolitan Area Network), a project where Telenor in cooperation with Datametrix established a service model covering operation of data, networks and sales of equipment’s for The City of Oslo. Close cooperation with the customer was the key factor for making the project a success. Test was important for establishing good processes and procedures to serve The City of Oslo.
